# Contributing

Thanks for helping improve Awesome Music Discovery.

## Suggest a resource

Open an issue or pull request with:

- **Name and link**
- **Short description**
- **Best for:** the specific discovery task it handles well
- **Limitations:** an important tradeoff, restriction, or weak spot
- **Category:** where it belongs in the current task-first structure

Use your own words. Do not copy product marketing text.

## Quality guidelines

A resource should:

1. Help people discover music to listen to.
2. Be usable or meaningfully informative today.
3. Offer a clear reason to recommend it for a particular task.
4. Have an honest limitation alongside its strengths.

This is a curated directory, not a comprehensive catalog. Submissions may be
declined when they duplicate a stronger entry, fall outside the scope, or
cannot be reasonably evaluated.

## Corrections and disagreements

Discovery is subjective. If an entry is inaccurate, stale, or unfair, open an
issue and explain:

- what should change;
- why it should change; and
- any source or firsthand experience supporting the correction.

Constructive disagreement is welcome. Promotion, referral links, and spam are
not.
